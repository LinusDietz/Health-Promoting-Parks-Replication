Examining Inequality in Park Quality for Promoting Health

Scores close to zero (colored in white) means a park has average offerings for the activities. If a park has a positive score, its offerings for doing health-promoting activities is better than average. A negative score (colored in grey to black), on the other hand, means the park is lacking in facilities for this activity. It's important to note that each city's scores are adjusted relative that city. The scores don't allow for a comparison between parks in different cities.
